Create beautiful things with fabric scraps

We are only using natural fabrics because of its good quality and properties.

Linen


Linen is a textile made from the stalks of the flax plant, which is an old cultivated plant that has been used for many years for its good properties. Linen has a high tensile strength in both dry and wet conditions but is not very stretchy. The textile has a high absorption capacity of moisture, an antiseptic capacity and feels cooling and dry against the skin. A textile that is well suited for accessories, clothing and interior decoration.

Wool


Wool has a number of good properties and, like many natural materials, wool breathes very well. Perhaps the most well-known feature is that the wool keeps you warm in a nice way. Wool warms even when wet and is a good choice for clothes and accessories for the colder half of the year. Wool is also dirt repellent and usually spot washing a stain is enough to get it clean. Perfect for your interior designs!
